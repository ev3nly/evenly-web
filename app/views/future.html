We're proud to announce that
Evenly is joining Square!

Over the past 18 months, we've been honored to help our users pay friends, fund vacations, share adventures, and much, much more. Along the way, we've been fueled by the belief that simplifying everyday payment experiences can help people better enjoy life's moments. So, we couldn't imagine a better place to continue our journey than at Square.

At Square, we'll be able to help millions of consumers and merchants, and tackle huge challenges alongside some of the most talented people in the industry. Together, we'll continue to bring simplicity, transparency, and immediacy to your commerce experiences. We're thrilled about the opportunity ahead!

Thank you for giving Evenly your trust and letting us move your money. It's been an honor! As part of joining Square, we'll be closing Evenly, but, rest assured, you'll have plenty of time to deposit your balances (learn more).

We hope you continue the journey with us and check out Square Cash, Wallet, Register and Market!

Best,
The Evenly Team
