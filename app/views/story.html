We believe that by simplifying everyday payment experiences, we can help people better enjoy life’s moments.

Vacations, BBQs, and dinners are more fun when you’re not worrying about money. Despite the presence of PayPal and others, daily financial interactions still cause unnecessary frustration and stress. We’re trying to fix that.

In January 2013, Evenly (then called Ivy) officially launched at Duke University. While there were many services that helped people send money, we focused on helping folks collect it. After all, collecting money from friends is always a burdensome task; so much so, that many experiences are avoided altogether because of it.

Within a month of launch, Evenly spread across campus, facilitating $100,000 in transaction volume. And that’s just the beginning!

Since then, our team has been working tirelessly to make it even easier to send and collect money for just about anything. We want our users to actually enjoy their payment experiences – not an easy task when most people loathe dealing with money.

What you see today is just the beginning. We’re building a series of new features and services we know you’ll love.  (After all, the ideas came from users just like you!)

We can’t share all of our secrets, so stay tuned. We’ll be releasing many updates over the coming months.  Soon, paying a friend will make you smile!
