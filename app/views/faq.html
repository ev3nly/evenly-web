What will happen to my Evenly account?

Your account will remain active until January 15, 2014, at which point we will be shutting down Evenly. Once Evenly is closed, your information – including card numbers and account details – will be deleted. If you still have an account balance after January 15, we will deposit your funds directly to your bank account.

If you have any questions, please email support@evenly.com.

What functionality is still available?

You can access your account and deposit any remaining Evenly balances. You will no longer be able to download the Evenly app, and we have disabled all new user registrations, and the ability to send and request funds.

How do I move money to my bank account?


Using the Website
  1. Select your Evenly Wallet in the top right corner of the screen.
  2. If you haven't done so already, quickly add a bank account.
  3. On the right side of screen click the Deposit button.
  4. Enter the amount
  5. Click Make Deposit to confirm.
Using the iPhone App
  1. From the home screen, select the Evenly Wallet located in the top right corner of the navigation bar or swipe the home screen to the left.
  2. If you haven't done so already, quickly add a bank account.
  3. Within the right panel, click the Deposit button. The large bold number on the top of the screen is the amount of money available for deposit.
  4. Enter the amount
  5. Select which bank account you would like the money deposited into by selecting the "Into my..." section.
  6. Tap the Deposit button at the bottom of the screen to confirm.

It will take approximately 1-2 business days for the funds to arrive in your bank account. Please note, the deposit will arrive from our partner, Balanced.
